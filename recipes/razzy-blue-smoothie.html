Razzy-Blue-Smoothie

The perfect breakfast smoothie to wake those taste buds up and keep you full of energy

The Razzy-Blue-Smoothie is our take on a berry classic.
It's packed with fruity goodness, healthy, and functional.
A recipe that's sure to get you started on the right foot!

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 16 whole almonds
  • 1/4 cup rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon flaxseed meal
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 cup rasberry yogurt
  • 1/4 cup Concord grape juice
  • 1 cup 1% buttermilk

Steps

  1. Peel the banana and cut into 1/2-inch chunks. Chill in freezer until solid, about 2 hours.
  2. Place the almonds, oats, and flaxseed meal into a blender; pulse until finely ground. Add the frozen banana, frozen blueberries, yogurt, grape juice, and buttermilk; puree until smooth.
