Fluffy French Toast

A breakfast classic, how you can cook the perfect french toast

French toast is a classic breakfast dish and with this one simple trick, you'll be making the perfect french toast in no time.
That simple trick is flour. Add a little bit of flour into the dish and you'll have a fluffier and fuller looking Frenchtoast.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 12 thick slices bread

Steps

  1. Measure flour into a large mixing bowl. Slowly whisk in milk. Whisk in eggs, sugar, vanilla extract, cinnamon, and salt until smooth.
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Soak bread slices in milk mixture until saturated.
  4. Working in batches, cook bread on the preheated griddle or pan until golden brown on each side. Serve hot.
